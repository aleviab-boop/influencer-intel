# Design Partner Outreach + LOI

**Goal.** Secure 5 design-partner LOIs from D2C brands. Free 6-month access in exchange for weekly feedback + case study rights. <5 LOIs = wedge isn't sharp enough; re-think positioning.

---

## When to ask

End of customer discovery call (Section 7 of `customer-discovery-script.md`). Only if at least 2 are true:

- They volunteered a specific pain point unprompted
- They asked "when does this launch?" or similar
- They named a frustration with a current tool
- They're running ≥2 campaigns per quarter (active buyer, not curious bystander)

If none of these, don't ask. Send a follow-up email when product is ready.

---

## The Ask (verbal, on call)

> One last thing — I'm shortlisting 5 brands as design partners for the closed beta. You'd get free access for 6 months once we ship, in exchange for ~30 minutes of weekly feedback and rights to use you as a case study. No money, no commitment to buy after. Would that be interesting?

If yes:

> Great. I'll send a one-page LOI tomorrow — non-binding, just outlines what we'd both get. Cool to send to [email]?

## LOI Template (one page)

**LETTER OF INTENT — DESIGN PARTNER PROGRAM**

Between **[Brand Pvt Ltd]** ("Brand") and **[Influencer Intel Pvt Ltd]** ("Company")

**Purpose**
Brand will participate as a design partner for Company's influencer-intelligence SaaS product (the "Platform") during its closed beta period.

**Term**
6 months of free access starting from [date Platform is ready for partner use], extendable by mutual agreement.

**What Company provides**

- Full Platform access for up to 5 Brand users
- Weekly 30-minute priority feedback calls with founders
- Direct input on roadmap and features prioritised in this period
- Locked-in pricing of 50% off published list rates for the 12 months following the design partner period

**What Brand provides**

- Honest, structured feedback on Platform usability and value
- Approximately 30 minutes of feedback per week
- Permission for Company to reference Brand as a design partner publicly (case study + logo) after Brand approves the specific case study content
- No obligation to purchase after the design partner period

**Confidentiality**
Both parties agree to keep non-public information confidential. Brand campaign data shared with Company will not be disclosed to third parties without consent.

**Non-binding**
This LOI is non-binding except for the confidentiality provision. Either party may withdraw with 14 days written notice.

**Signed**

[Brand signatory] [Date]
[Company signatory] [Date]
