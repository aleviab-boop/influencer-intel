## Weekly cadence

Every Friday — founder fills in:

- What moved this week (specific progress per row)
- What's blocked (need to unblock before Monday)
- One quote from a discovery call

---

## Red-flag escalation

If at any check-in, any of these are true → **stop and re-think positioning**:

- <8 discovery calls completed after 4 weeks of effort (sales motion is too slow)
- 0 LOIs signed after 10+ calls (wedge isn't sharp)
- Data prototype <60% accuracy (data plan needs major rework)
- Pricing OPP >2× off target (positioning is wrong)

Don't push through with a thin foundation. The integrated build is one push — you can't recover from a wrong foundation by phasing the build.
